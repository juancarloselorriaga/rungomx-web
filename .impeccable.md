# RungoMX Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
**Event organizers** — primarily experienced or repeat organizers who are time-pressured and stressed. They're not exploring; they have a job to finish. The AI wizard is a tool they reach for when they need to get something done fast, not a feature they play with. They know their event; they need the platform to keep up with them. Occasional first-timers exist but the primary mental model is "busy professional under deadline."

**Jobs to be done:**
- Fill in event details quickly and correctly
- Trust that the AI suggestions are accurate and safe to apply
- Move from step to step without cognitive overhead
- Feel in control — not guided, but empowered

**Emotional goals:** Confidence, speed, relief. Not delight, not wonder — just "this works, I got it done."

### Brand Personality
**Bold. Modern. Community-driven.**

RungoMX is the platform for the Mexican running community. It's not a generic SaaS tool — it has energy and identity. The design should feel like it belongs to a sport: purposeful, forward-moving, a little athletic. It takes organizing seriously without being corporate.

Voice: Direct, respectful, peer-to-peer. Talks to organizers as fellow community members, not customers.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Visual tone:** Elevated above the dashboard — a flagship feature, slightly premium. Not jarring, but noticeably refined.

**Color system:** oklch-based, warm. Primary is a warm orange-red. Brand blue and green for accents. Gold is reserved for Pro tier. No neon, no glow, no dark-mode tech aesthetic.

**Typography:** Geist Sans — modern, humanist, variable weight. Lean on weight variation and size contrast for hierarchy. No decorative fonts needed.

**Theme:** Light mode primary. The platform reads clean and professional in daylight — the context where organizers are doing event prep work.

**Anti-references (what this must NOT look like):**
- Generic ChatGPT / raw LLM chat UI — no featureless bubble chat
- Clippy-style over-eager assistant energy — no "Hi! I'm here to help!" cheerfulness
- Dark AI aesthetic — no cyan-on-dark, glowing borders, purple-blue gradients, neon accents
- Corporate SaaS wizard — no sterile step-through progress bars that feel like a DMV form

### Design Principles

1. **Speed over ceremony.** Every interaction should feel fast. No loading spinners where skeleton states work. No confirmation dialogs where optimistic UI works. The organizer's time is the most valuable resource.

2. **Confidence through clarity.** The AI proposes, the organizer decides. Proposals should be scannable at a glance — what changed, why, what the impact is. No walls of text.

3. **Elevated, not alien.** The AI wizard is a premium layer on top of the existing design system. It uses the same tokens but applies them with more intention — tighter spacing, stronger hierarchy, more considered states. It should feel like a flagship feature, not a different product.

4. **Community, not corporate.** Tone is peer-to-peer. The interface has energy without being loud. It feels like it was designed by someone who runs, not by a B2B SaaS team.

5. **Control belongs to the organizer.** The AI assists; it never takes over. Apply/dismiss/edit controls must always be visible and obvious. The organizer should never wonder "wait, did that just change my event?"
